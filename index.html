About Us

Our Story & Mission

Est.
2017

Built on Trust, Grown with Purpose

Founded in 2017 at Nalkur, Udupi District, Surabhi Feeds began with a vision to transform cattle nutrition in coastal Karnataka. What started as a small operation has grown into a respected name among dairy farmers across six districts.

We understand the challenges faced by Indian dairy farmers — inconsistent milk yield, cattle health issues, and access to quality nutrition. Our products are our answer to those challenges.

Our Mission

To manufacture and deliver scientifically formulated, high-quality cattle feed that maximizes milk production while keeping cattle healthy and strong.

Our Vision

To be Karnataka's most trusted cattle feed brand, empowering every dairy farmer with the nutrition their herd deserves.
